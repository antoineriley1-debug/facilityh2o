# FacilityH2O Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 2, 2026

---

## Overview

FacilityH2O ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and users. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to your data.

**Important Notice:** FacilityH2O is a water chemistry compliance tracking platform. We do **not** collect, store, or process Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined under HIPAA. We are not a Covered Entity under HIPAA. This Service is used in clinical and healthcare environments for facility infrastructure management only — not for patient care, clinical systems, or medical records.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Water Chemistry Data
- Chemistry readings (pH, chlorine, temperature, conductivity, biological test results, etc.)
- Test locations and equipment identifiers
- Timestamps and frequency of readings
- Compliance notes and corrective actions
- Historical trend data

This is the core operational data of the Service. It relates to building infrastructure and water systems — **not patients, residents, or clinical subjects**.

### 1.2 User Account Information
- Full name
- Work email address
- Job title / role
- Organization / facility name
- Password (hashed; we never store plaintext passwords)
- Account creation date and last login

### 1.3 Facility and Organization Information
- Facility name, address, and type (hospital, hotel, commercial building, etc.)
- Building system descriptions (cooling towers, domestic hot water, etc.)
- Number of facilities in account
- Organizational hierarchy / department structure

### 1.4 Billing Information
- Billing contact name and email
- Subscription plan and payment history
- **Note:** Full credit card numbers and payment details are handled exclusively by Stripe. We do not store raw payment card data.

### 1.5 Usage and Technical Data
- IP addresses and browser/device type
- Pages visited and features used within the Service
- Error logs and performance diagnostics
- Session duration and click-through patterns

## 2. What We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do **not** collect or store:

- **Patient names, dates of birth, or any patient identifiers**
- **Medical records or clinical data of any kind**
- **Protected Health Information (PHI)** as defined by HIPAA
- **Social Security Numbers**
- **Government-issued ID numbers**
- Personal financial data beyond billing contact information

If you believe you have accidentally entered PHI into FacilityH2O, contact us immediately at antoine.riley@crothall.com for removal assistance.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- **Deliver the Service:** Process and store your chemistry readings, generate reports, and display compliance dashboards.
- **Alerts and Notifications:** Send automated email alerts when readings fall outside configured thresholds.
- **Trend Analysis:** Generate historical trend reports and predictive insights about your water systems.
- **Account Management:** Authenticate users, manage permissions, and maintain account records.
- **Billing:** Process subscription payments and send invoices via Stripe and Resend.
- **Customer Support:** Diagnose issues and respond to support requests.
- **Service Improvement:** Analyze aggregate, anonymized usage data to improve features and performance.
- **Legal Compliance:** Maintain records as required by applicable laws and regulations.

We do **not** sell your data to third parties. We do not use your operational chemistry data for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Data Retention

- **Chemistry readings and operational data:** Retained for a minimum of **24 months** (2 years) from the date of entry, consistent with ASHRAE 188 and The Joint Commission water management documentation standards. Customers on custom Enterprise agreements may negotiate longer retention periods.
- **User account data:** Retained for the duration of the active subscription plus 30 days post-cancellation to allow data export.
- **Billing records:** Retained for 7 years as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.
- **Usage logs:** Retained for 90 days for security and diagnostic purposes.

After the applicable retention period, data is permanently deleted from production systems and backups within 60 days.

## 7. Security Measures

We implement the following security controls to protect your data:

- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher.
- **Encryption at rest:** Customer Data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
- **Access controls:** Strict role-based access controls. Employees access only what is necessary for their job function.
- **Authentication:** Multi-factor authentication (MFA) available and recommended for all accounts.
- **Password security:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage.
- **Vulnerability management:** Regular security assessments and dependency updates.
- **Incident response:** Documented breach response procedures (see Section 8).

No system is 100% secure. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact us immediately.
