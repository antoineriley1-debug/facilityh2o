## 2. Description of Service

FacilityH2O is a cloud-based Software as a Service (SaaS) platform designed for water chemistry compliance tracking in commercial, healthcare, and hospitality environments. The Service enables facilities to:

- Log, track, and analyze water chemistry readings
- Monitor compliance with applicable water management standards (including ASHRAE 188 and The Joint Commission requirements)
- Generate alerts for out-of-range chemistry values
- Produce compliance reports and trend analyses
- Manage water treatment programs across multiple facilities

The Service is intended for use by facility managers, water treatment professionals, engineers, and compliance staff. It is **not** a clinical system and does not interface with patient care systems.
